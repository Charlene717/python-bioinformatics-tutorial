pandas read_csv 在 Windows vs Linux 的編碼預設差異

pandas read_csv() 的 encoding 參數預設為 None，實際解碼會交由 Python 的 locale.getpreferredencoding(False) 決定：Windows 上多為 cp950 / cp1252，Linux/macOS 通常是 utf-8。同一份含中文或重音字的 CSV 在 Windows 直接 open 會 UnicodeDecodeError，在 Linux 卻正常。建議所有 I/O 一律明寫 encoding="utf-8"（或 "utf-8-sig" 處理 Excel 匯出的 BOM），別依賴系統 locale。Python 3.15（PEP 597）將預設 text mode 改為 UTF-8，但 pandas 不受 PEP 597 影響。

The same CSV containing Chinese or accented characters will UnicodeDecodeError on Windows but read fine on Linux. Always pass encoding="utf-8" explicitly (or "utf-8-sig" to handle the BOM emitted by Excel) — never rely on the system locale. Python 3.15 (PEP 597) flips the default text-mode encoding to UTF-8, but pandas is not governed by PEP 597.

pandas merge 預設 suffix _x / _y 容易造成欄位名稱遺失

當兩個 DataFrame 有同名非 key 欄位，pd.merge() 預設加上 suffixes=("_x","_y")。在 RNA-seq 樣本 metadata 合併基因表達矩陣時，sample_id_x / sample_id_y 常被誤以為是新欄位，下游 groupby 也會出錯。建議：(1) merge 前先 rename() 兩邊衝突欄位；或 (2) 明寫 suffixes=("_meta","_expr") 讓出處清楚；或 (3) 用 validate="one_to_one" 強制檢查 key 唯一性，從根本減少誤合併。

When two DataFrames share non-key column names, pd.merge() silently appends suffixes=("_x","_y") by default. When merging RNA-seq sample metadata with an expression matrix, sample_id_x / sample_id_y are often mistaken for new columns and break downstream groupby. Best practices: (1) rename() conflicting columns before merging; (2) pass explicit suffixes=("_meta","_expr") so origins are obvious; or (3) use validate="one_to_one" to enforce key uniqueness and prevent silent fan-out.

Biopython SeqRecord.id 會在第一個空白處被截斷

BLAST 的 E-value 不是機率：別寫成 p-value

BLAST 報表中的 E-value 是「在當前資料庫大小下，期望（expected）出現分數至少與當前 hit 相同的隨機 alignment 數」，是泊松過程的期望值 λ，可大於 1（如 E=15 表示預期會有 15 個更好或同等的隨機命中）。對應的機率為 p = 1 − exp(−E)，當 E < 0.01 時 p ≈ E，因此 E 小時可近似當機率使用，但在 E > 0.1 時把 E-value 當 p-value 引用是錯誤；E-value 也不應做 Bonferroni / BH 校正，它已內含資料庫大小的多重比較修正。Altschul et al. 1990 與 Karlin–Altschul statistics 為標準參考。

The E-value reported by BLAST is the expected number of random alignments scoring at least as high as the current hit given the current database size — it is the Poisson rate λ and can exceed 1 (e.g. E = 15 means about 15 equally-good or better random hits are expected). The corresponding probability is p = 1 − exp(−E); for E < 0.01 we have p ≈ E so the E-value can be used as a probability approximation, but quoting E-values above 0.1 as p-values is wrong. E-values should also not be Bonferroni- or BH-corrected — the database size multiple-testing correction is already built in. See Altschul et al. 1990 and the Karlin–Altschul statistics for the canonical treatment.

pysam 是 0-based half-open，與 VCF / SAM 文字格式（1-based）差 1

pysam 沿用 BAM/HTSlib 的內部座標：read.reference_start、fetch(chrom, start, end) 全為 0-based half-open（包含 start、不含 end，類似 BED）。但你在 IGV 或 VCF 看到的位置是 1-based inclusive（VCF spec），SAM 文字檔的 POS 欄位也是 1-based。常見錯誤：在 pileup 報告 SNV 時把 pileupcolumn.reference_pos 直接寫成 VCF 的 POS，會少 1。轉換規則：VCF POS = pysam pos + 1；BED start = pysam start、BED end = pysam end（相同）。pyranges、HTSeq 等套件也採 0-based，但 bcftools / tabix 的 region 字串為 1-based。

pysam follows the internal coordinates of BAM/HTSlib: read.reference_start and fetch(chrom, start, end) are all 0-based half-open (start inclusive, end exclusive, like BED). However, the positions you see in IGV or VCF are 1-based inclusive (VCF spec), and the POS column in SAM text files is also 1-based. A common bug: writing pileupcolumn.reference_pos directly as the VCF POS in pileup-based SNV reports — it will be off by one. Conversions: VCF POS = pysam pos + 1; BED start/end = pysam start/end (same). pyranges and HTSeq are also 0-based, but bcftools / tabix region strings are 1-based.

VCF normalization：合併與比較前必須 left-align + split multi-allelic（Tan 2015）

同一個 indel 可有多種等價表示（例如 chr1:100 ATG>A 與 chr1:101 TG>-），不同 caller（GATK HaplotypeCaller、DeepVariant、bcftools）輸出未必相同；multi-allelic 位點 A>G,T 也常被當成單一變異處理。Tan et al. 2015 提出 normalization 三步驟：(1) parsimony（最小化共同前後綴）、(2) left-alignment（往左對齊到最早可能位置）、(3) split multi-allelic 為多列 bi-allelic。標準指令：bcftools norm -f ref.fa -m -any input.vcf。未經 normalization 的 VCF 在 merge、annotation（如 VEP、SnpEff）、liftOver 與 PRS 計算都會出現假性不一致。

The same indel can have several equivalent representations (e.g. chr1:100 ATG>A and chr1:101 TG>-), and different callers (GATK HaplotypeCaller, DeepVariant, bcftools) do not necessarily produce identical strings; multi-allelic sites A>G,T are often treated as a single record. Tan et al. 2015 defined a three-step normalization: (1) parsimony (trim common prefix and suffix), (2) left-alignment (shift to the leftmost equivalent position), (3) split multi-allelics into separate bi-allelic records. Canonical command: bcftools norm -f ref.fa -m -any input.vcf. Without normalization, VCF merges, annotations (VEP, SnpEff), liftOver, and PRS calculations produce spurious mismatches.

補充說明 · Notes

AlphaFold pLDDT 不等同實驗結構解析度

AlphaFold2 / AlphaFold3 在 B-factor 欄位輸出的是 per-residue pLDDT（0–100），表示對該殘基局部結構的信心：>90 高信心、70–90 中等、50–70 低、<50 通常為內在無序區（IDR）。實驗結構的 B-factor 反映原子熱運動或晶體紊亂，單位 Å²，與 pLDDT 不同維度也不可直接比對。X-ray 的 1.5 Å resolution 與 AlphaFold 的 pLDDT=95 在意義上沒有對等關係；後者僅是「模型對自己預測的信心」，不能用來宣稱「實驗等級準確度」。AlphaFold-Multimer 預測介面時還需看 ipTM / PAE，pLDDT 對複合體界面有系統性高估。Jumper et al. 2021 與 Abramson et al. 2024 為原始定義。

AlphaFold2 / AlphaFold3 store per-residue pLDDT (0–100) in the B-factor column to indicate confidence in the local structure: >90 high, 70–90 moderate, 50–70 low, <50 typically intrinsically disordered regions (IDRs). Experimental B-factors measure atomic thermal motion or crystal disorder in Å² — a different quantity that cannot be compared directly. There is no equivalence between a 1.5 Å X-ray resolution and an AlphaFold pLDDT of 95; the latter is only "the model's confidence in its own prediction" and does not justify claims of "experimental-grade accuracy". For interfaces, AlphaFold-Multimer requires ipTM and PAE; pLDDT systematically overestimates confidence at complex interfaces. The original definitions are in Jumper et al. 2021 and Abramson et al. 2024.
